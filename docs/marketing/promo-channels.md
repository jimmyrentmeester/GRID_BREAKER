# Promo channels — GRID_BREAKER

Where to post at launch. Copy is ready in `launch-copy.md`; visuals in
`docs/marketing/social/` (cards, `clip-square.gif`, `clip-story.mp4`) and the App
Preview in `docs/preview/`. **Golden rule:** lead with value, follow each community's
self-promo rules, and reply to every comment — don't blast the same post everywhere at
once. Links/rules change, so double-check each before posting.

## Tier 1 — do on launch day
| Channel | What to post | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Personal IG** (feed + story) | OUT NOW card + `clip-square.gif` / `clip-story.mp4` | Your warmest audience — use the personal caption. |
| **LinkedIn** | The "I shipped something" post | App Store link in the **first comment**, not the post. |
| **r/iOSGaming** | Launch post + GIF | Read the rules; they allow dev self-posts but want substance. |
| **r/playmygame** | Launch post + clip | Built for this; expect to play others' games back. |
| **r/iosapps** | Short launch post | Free app, no-tracking angle plays well. |
| **Product Hunt** | Full PH listing (tagline + maker comment) | Schedule for **00:01 PT** to get a full day of votes. |

## Tier 2 — the "I built this" angle (great for an indie solo dev)
| Channel | What to post | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Hacker News** | "Show HN: GRID_BREAKER — a neon reflex iPhone game, fully on-device" | Lead with the engineering story (deterministic engine, code-synth audio, no backend). |
| **iOS Dev Weekly** (submit link) | Link + one line | Dave Verwer's newsletter; submit via iosdevweekly.com. Dev-build angle. |
| **Indie iOS Focus Weekly** | Submit your launch | Indie-focused newsletter; accepts submissions. |
| **Mastodon (iosdev.space) / Bluesky** | Clip + `#indiedev #iOSdev #gamedev` | The iOS dev crowd lives here; friendly to launches. |

## Tier 3 — gameplay-clip platforms (you now have a clip)
| Channel | What | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **TikTok / IG Reels / YT Shorts** | `clip-story.mp4` (9:16) | Short neon gameplay loops do well; add the launch caption + link in bio. |
| **r/indiegames, r/IndieDev** | Clip + short blurb | Showcase culture; engage back. |

## Tier 4 — press (low hit-rate, but free; send a short, personal pitch)
Keep it 4–5 sentences: what it is, the hook (solo-built, no ads/tracking, free), a link,
and the App Preview/screenshots attached or linked.
- **TouchArcade** — forums "Upcoming/Released" thread + news tip.
- **AppAdvice**, **Cult of Mac** (tips@cultofmac.com), **9to5Mac** (tips@9to5mac.com).
- **Pocket Gamer** — "submit news" form.

## Cadence (suggested)
- **Now (pre-launch):** post the **teaser** card to IG + a LinkedIn teaser. Tease once,
  maybe twice — don't overdo it.
- **Launch day:** Tier 1 + Tier 2, spread across the day (not all at 9am).
- **Days 2–7:** Tier 3 clips; reply everywhere; thank early reviewers.
- Ask friends/family to **download + rate in the first 48h** — early velocity helps
  discovery more than anything else for a brand-new app.

## Don't
- Don't paste identical text everywhere (communities notice; some auto-remove).
- Don't argue with critics — thank, note, move on.
- Don't buy installs/reviews — it risks the account and skews nothing useful.
