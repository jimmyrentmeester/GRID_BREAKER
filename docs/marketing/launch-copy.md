## Product Hunt
**Tagline (≤60):** `Neon reflex grid-hacking — one thumb, zero tracking`

**Description:**
```
GRID_BREAKER is a fast neon reflex game for iPhone. Jack into the grid, decode the
daemons before they vanish, dodge the firewalls, and chain clean hits into Fever as the
pace climbs. Four modes (Endless, a 10-core Campaign, calm Flow, and a shared Daily Hack),
```

```
Hey PH 👋 I built GRID_BREAKER solo. I wanted a reflex game that respects you: no ads, no
```
